If you want to test the circuit. Whenever you open either datapath files (single-cycle or pipeline), do the following
1- Left click on "registerFile" in the circuit. You should find it near the center of the circuit.
2- On the sidebar on the left side of the screen, you should see the word "Properties"
3- In properties, search for the property "Appearance"
4- Left click on the option beside "Appearance". It's most likely to be "Logisim-Evolution"
5- Upon clicking, you should get a list of options. Choose "Custom"

Done. Now you can insert your code in ROM and play with the design.
